Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) aims to provide affordable housing in urban areas of India.
It focuses on slum redevelopment, credit-linked subsidies, affordable rental housing, and women empowerment.
The scheme also promotes infrastructure development, green and sustainable housing, public-private partnerships, and the use of technology and innovation.
With a goal of inclusive development, PMAY-U caters to the housing needs of economically weaker sections, lower-income groups, and middle-income groups.
By providing financial assistance, basic amenities, and promoting collaboration among stakeholders, PMAY-U strives to ensure that all eligible beneficiaries have access to decent and affordable housing.

Eligibility Criteria :

For : For people who don't own any house anywhere in country
Gender : Male,Female
State :: All Urban Areas
Cast : For all
Annual Income :EWS - upto ₹3,00,000
                            LIG - between ₹3,00,000 - ₹6,00,000
                            MIG 1 - between ₹6,00,000 - ₹12,00,000
                            MIG 2 - between ₹12,00,000 - ₹18,00,000

Required Documents:

  • Proof of Identity: Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, PAN card
  • Proof of Residence: Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, utility bills (electricity/water)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Application Form
  • Property Documents: If already owning a property, relevant documents like property registration papers, ownership proof, etc.

    • Pedalandariki Illu

    The objective of the Pedalandariki Illu scheme in Andhra Pradesh is to provide housing to the homeless and those living in vulnerable conditions.
    It aims to reduce homelessness and uplift the socially disadvantaged by offering them safe and secure housing.
    The scheme contributes to poverty alleviation efforts and empowers individuals and families by providing them with a sense of dignity and stability.
    By addressing the housing needs of the marginalized, the Pedalandariki Illu scheme aims to improve their quality of life, promote social welfare, and create a more inclusive and equitable society in Andhra Pradesh.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : For people who does not have their own house
    Gender : Male,Female
    State : Andhra Pradesh
    Cast : For all
    Annual Income : 3 Lakh

    Required Documents:

  • Passport Size Photo
  • Aadhar Card
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook Copy
  • Ration Card

    • NIRMAN SHRAMIK PUCCA GHAR YOJANA

    The Nirman Shramik Pucca Ghar Yojana aims to provide permanent houses to construction workers and their families.
    Its objectives include addressing the housing needs of construction workers, empowering them, and ensuring social security.
    The scheme focuses on offering affordable housing options to construction workers, improving their living conditions, and enhancing their overall well-being.
    It may also provide livelihood support through skill development programs and employment opportunities.
    By promoting the construction of pucca houses, the scheme contributes to infrastructure development, creating a positive impact on the lives of construction workers and their families.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : Construction Workers, registered under the Odisha Building & Other Construction Workers Welfare Board (OB&OCWWB)
    Gender : Male,Female
    State : Odisha
    Cast :SEBC
    Annual Income : 2 Lakh 50K

    Required Documents:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Ration Card
  • Residence certificate
  • Income certificate
  • Proof of age
  • Bank details

    • GITANJALI HOUSING SCHEME

    The objective of the GITANJALI HOUSING SCHEME in West Bengal is to provide housing to the homeless and those living in vulnerable conditions.
    The Government in the Department of housing has decided to modify the housing scheme for new construction of houses throughout the state of West Bengal for Economically weaker section of people (EWS) who have no pucca house of thier own or in the name of any of their family.
    The house would be constructed by beneficiaries themselves and no contracting agency will be engened for the purpose.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : Poor People in Rural and Urban areas
    Gender :Male,Female
    State : West Bengal
    Cast : All
    Annual Income : ₹72,000

    Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar card, voter ID, PAN card, passport, or driving license
  • Electricity bill, water bill, or telephone bill
  • Income certificate
  • Bank account details
  • Photographs of the applicant
  • Birth certificate, school leaving certificate, or passport

    • Sardar Aavas Yojana

    The Sardar Aavas Yojana is a government scheme in India with the objective of providing affordable housing to economically weaker sections.
    It aims to empower beneficiaries by improving their living conditions and promoting inclusive development.
    The scheme focuses on rehabilitating slum dwellers and stimulating infrastructure development, creating employment opportunities.
    It also facilitates access to housing finance and promotes sustainable construction practices.
    By providing affordable housing, the scheme enhances the overall quality of life and social well-being of marginalized sections of society.
    It contributes to urban planning by developing well-planned housing colonies and townships, ensuring proper land utilization and resource management.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : Land less agricultural laburers and village artisan living Below Poverty line in rural areas of the State
    Gender : Male, Female
    State : Gujarat
    Cast : For all
    Annual Income : ₹27,000

    Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc
  • Address proof such as ration card, electricity bill, etc
  • Land documents to prove that the applicant does not own a house or plot
  • Certificate from the Sarpanch of the native village stating that the applicant has not previously taken advantage of the Sardar Patel Awas Yojana and meets the B.P.L criteria
  • Income certificates
