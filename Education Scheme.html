Gujarat Vahli Dikri Yojana

The objective of the Gujarat Vahli Dikri Yojana is to address gender inequality, promote the value of girl children, and ensure their holistic development.
It aims to encourage the birth and upbringing of girls by providing financial assistance and educational incentives to parents. The scheme aims to increase the enrollment of girls in schools, reduce dropout rates, and empower them through skill development and vocational training.
It also focuses on creating awareness about the importance of girl child education and aims to change societal perceptions. Ultimately, the scheme strives to uplift the status of girl children, promote their education, and empower them to become self-reliant individuals.

Eligibility Criteria :

For : Women and Girl Child of Gujarat
Gender : Female
State : Gujarat
Cast : For all
Annual Income : 2 Lakh

Required Documents:

  • Application Form
  • Domicile of Gujarat
  • Aadhar card
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Birth Certificate of Child
  • Bank Account Details
  • Affidavit

    • RTE Gujarat Admission

    The objective of RTE Gujarat Admission is to ensure free and compulsory education for children from economically and socially disadvantaged backgrounds in the state of Gujarat, India.
    The scheme aims to provide equal opportunities for education and bridge the gap between different sections of society.
    It focuses on enrolling eligible children in private schools, reserving a percentage of seats for them.
    The scheme seeks to eliminate discrimination and improve access to quality education for children belonging to marginalized communities.
    By implementing RTE Gujarat Admission, the government aims to promote inclusive education, uplift underprivileged children, and create a more equitable and just educational system in the state.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : All caste, student’s age must be above 5 Years and below 7 Years
    Gender : Male,Female
    State : Gujarat
    Cast : For all
    Annual Income : 1 Lakh

    Required Documents:

  • Student Recent Passport Size Photo
  • Aadhar Card of Student
  • Parent’s Aadhaar Card and other ID
  • Student Birth Certificate
  • One Address Proof
  • School Admission Receipt
  • Family Income Certificate
  • Self-Attested Document
  • Bank Passbook Copy
  • BPL Ration Card

    • Instrumental Assistance for Medical, Engineering, Diploma Students

    The objective of Instrumental Assistance for Medical, Engineering, and Diploma Students is to ensure equal access to quality education in the fields of medical, engineering, and diploma courses.
    It aims to support talented and deserving students who may face financial constraints in pursuing their chosen fields of study.
    The assistance program seeks to remove financial barriers and provide resources such as scholarships, grants, or loans to cover educational expenses.
    By providing this support, the objective is to empower students to pursue their educational aspirations, develop their skills, and contribute to the development of the healthcare, engineering, and technical sectors.
    It aims to create a skilled workforce and promote social and economic progress through education and professional opportunities in these specialized fields.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : Be studying in medical/engineering or diploma courses
    Gender : Male,Female
    State : Gujarat
    Cast :SEBC
    Annual Income : 2 Lakh 50K

    Required Documents:

  • caste certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • bank passbook/cancelled cheque
  • Fee receipt of current academic course
  • previous academic mark sheet
  • Break affidavit (If break gap is more than one year)
  • Hostel certificate (only For hosteller student)
  • Income certificate
  • School leaving certificate
  • bonafide certificate

    • Chief Minister Scholarship Scheme

    The objectives of the Chief Minister Scholarship Scheme are to provide financial assistance and promote higher education among economically disadvantaged students.
    The scheme aims to support talented students who face financial constraints in pursuing their education.
    It provides scholarships to deserving students based on merit or need, covering tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses. The scheme encourages students to excel academically and achieve their educational goals.
    By offering financial support, the objective is to reduce the financial burden on students and their families, increase access to quality education, and empower students to contribute to the socio-economic development of the nation.
    It aims to create a skilled and educated workforce, fostering inclusive growth and equal opportunities for all.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : All the students who have secured at least 60% marks in the 10th or 12th board examination
    Gender :Male,Female
    State : Gujarat
    Cast : SC, ST, SEBC
    Annual Income : 4 Lakh 50K

    Required Documents:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Residence or Domicile Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Annual Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • College Fee Receipt
  • Latest Passport Size Photograph

    • Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

    The "Beti Bachao Beti Padhao" scheme is an initiative by the Government of India to promote the welfare and education of girls.
    The main goal of this scheme is to address the declining child sex ratio and empower girls by ensuring their survival, protection, and education.
    The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao scheme also involves implementing policies and programs to improve the overall status of girls, including access to education, healthcare, and nutrition. It promotes girls' enrollment in schools and colleges and provides scholarships and financial incentives to support their education.

    Eligibility Criteria :

    For : Girl child below 10 years of age
    Gender : Female
    State : All
    Cast : For all
    Annual Income : No criteria

    Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate of the applicant
  • Parent’s ID card
  • Aadhaar card
  • Address proof
  • Passport size photograph
